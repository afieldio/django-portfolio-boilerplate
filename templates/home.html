wings to websites

Hello! My name is Adam.

I am a Technical project manager who has delivered award winning products for Google and Saatchi & Saatchi. An advocate of agile thinking and lover of efficiency and process. I also like to dabble in code, have a life long ambition to become a polymath and think Aquaponics will help solve the food crisis.

Question is, how did I get here?

Adam the pilot

I started my career as a commercial pilot and have over 3000 hours on a Boeing 737-800. I learnt a lot about effective communication, leadership qualities and also that the devil is in the detail but you can't aford to lose sight of the bigger picture.

After nearly five years when my contract came to an end I was at one of those forks in life. I decided to take the red pill and haven't looked back!

Adam the student

After leaving aviation I went back to University to study for a BSc in web development. We covered a whole range of topics from UX/HCI, JAVA, JS, PHP. We even did some Action Script! For my final disertation I focused on learning some Python / C++ and built a Raspberry Pi and Arduino powered Aquaponic system.

Adam the project manager

I have been working at Potato for three years now. Starting as a junior PM I was promoted after a year to technical project manager. I have worked on a wide range of projects from bespoke project managment tools to overseeing the overhaul of a major website that recieves millions of hits a day.
