Who am I?

Manager, Developer, Pilot

I am a Project Manager for Digital Agency called Potato. I recently went back to University after a career as a commercial pilot. I gained a first class degree in Web Development where we studied a range of languages and other web related skills. It is great to be a part of this industry - lots of creative, inspiring people and enough variety to keep me on my toes!

What do I do?

Design

I can help bring your idea to life. I aim to get a good understanding of the client and users needs to create a solution that is bespoke, engaging and enjoyable.

Develop

I am confident with the basics (HTML, CSS and JS) and have experience with backend frameworks like Django and Symphony2. I also enjoy playing with my RPi!

Deploy

This site is deployed on a DigialOcean droplet so I have an understanding of basic server configuration and setup (Nginx, Gunicorn, Supervisor).

What drives me?

Challenge

I love a good challenge and there is nothing like the feeling of overcoming an obstacle. Neil Armstrong said, "I think we're going to the moon because it's in the nature of the human being to face challenges."

Learn

I think the greatest aim in life is to become a polymath! Going back to University at a later stage in life has given me a re-newed fascination with wanting to expand my knowledge base.

Build
